The global market for petrol station management software is expected to grow, driven by the increasing number of petrol stations and the need for more efficient and accurate management of operations. The market is also likely to see increased demand for automation and integration with other business systems and applications, as well as greater use of mobile and cloud-based solutions.

According to a report by MarketsandMarkets, the petrol station management system market was valued at USD 7.6 billion in 2020 and is expected to reach USD 15.8 billion by 2025, growing at a CAGR of 15.1% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by the increasing demand for automation and digitalization of petrol stations, the growing adoption of IoT and cloud-based solutions, and the need for real-time monitoring and analysis of petrol station operations.
