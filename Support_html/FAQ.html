Frequently Asked Questions

What is InDhanPay?

InDhanPay is a fintech company at CIDI, SGSITS, Indore, Madhya Pradesh. It is a new way of payment for an all-in-one integrated payment and management system for petroleum retail outlets.

What makes InDhanPay different from other payment gateways?

InDhanPay is different from other payment gateways because it specifically provides services for petroleum units.

Why is it beneficial for the dealership?

InDhanPay provides integrated payment management software for petroleum dealerships to manage their financial accounts with per-day sales data, card transactions, and UPI inventory. It joins all dealership accounts in the same portal. It gives the history of previous customers with vehicle RTO numbers, Non QR Vehicles verification, and payment invoice. There will be less dependency on management experts on each site. Truck refilling vehicle monitoring is also available.
