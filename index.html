About Us

InDhanPay is a Fintech startup listed at CIDI, (Centre for Innovation, Design and Incubation) at SGSITS, MP. InDhanPayGate is a novel and futuristic solution that is essentially a non-QR service for efficiently managing payments at fuel dealerships. The InDhanPay application is a specially designed payment portal for petroleum retail outlets all over India and /or overseas dealerships. Adding to the benefits, synchronising UPI (Unified payment Interface) with the dealership enables the dealer to reliably verify the account logs of its employees.

Our product is a payment gateway ecosystem designed to streamline the fuel sector & to facilitate secure and fast payments.
